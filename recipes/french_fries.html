French Fries

Description

In need of a crowd-pleasing appetizer, side dish, or snack? Use your trusty air fryer to make these irresistable French fries.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the Potatoes
  2. Soak and Drain the Potatoes
  3. Season the French Fries
  4. Cool in Air Fryer
